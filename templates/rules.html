Rules:

The rules of the game are simple: one song will be randomly selected from the Deezer playlist (the base playlist is Top 50 - Global). Your job is to guess which song it was from a selection of four options. For each correct answer, your score will increase. If you choose the wrong one, the game is over. The same song will never appear twice in a row.

Future changes:

Currently, this website offers only one game mode (singleplayer), but I have future plans to implement other modes, such as multiplayer — where multiple players compete against each other — and a custom mode, where the host can choose a Spotify playlist for the game (following certain rules) and decide whether to play in singleplayer or multiplayer mode.
